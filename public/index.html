Frequently Asked Questions




What makes Vibe Beads jewelry special? +
Every piece in our collection is carefully handcrafted using premium gemstones and materials. We source our beads from ethical suppliers and ensure each piece is unique, with slight variations that make your jewelry truly one-of-a-kind. Our jewelry is designed to be both beautiful and meaningful, incorporating stones with healing properties and positive energy.
How long do your candles burn? +
Our candles are made with premium soy wax that burns cleanly and slowly. A standard 8oz candle will burn for approximately 45-50 hours, while our larger 16oz candles can burn for up to 90 hours. We recommend trimming the wick to 1/4 inch before each use for optimal burn time and performance.
What can I do with finished candle containers? +
Our candle containers are designed to be repurposed! Once your candle is finished, clean out the remaining wax with hot water and use the beautiful glass containers as planters for small succulents, storage for jewelry or office supplies, or decorative vases. We also offer candle refills for local customers who want to reuse their favorite containers.
Are your products safe and non-toxic? +
Absolutely! Our candles are made with 100% soy wax, which burns cleanly without releasing harmful toxins. We use cotton wicks and phthalate-free fragrances. Our jewelry uses hypoallergenic materials and nickel-free components. All our products are cruelty-free and environmentally conscious.
